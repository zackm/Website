Raytracing

Assignment was to create a raytracer from scratch using C++ and OpenGL. Project parameters:

  • render spheres,ellipsoids, and triangles
  • diffuse, specular, ambient light
  • reflection and shadow
  • interpolation of triangle vertex normals


Full Scene with Reflection

Features: triangles, spheres, Phong shading, reflections, shadows, directional lights, transformation (rotate by 90), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 40 seconds

Ambient Only

Features: triangles, spheres, ambient only light, transformation (rotate by 90), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 23 seconds

Diffuse

Features: triangles, spheres, diffuse only light, shadows, directional lights, transformation (rotate by 90), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 24 seconds

Specular Only

Features: triangles, spheres, Specular only (no reflection), directional lights, transformation (rotate by 90), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 25 seconds

Depth 1 Reflection

Features: triangles, spheres, Ambient, Diffuse, Specular with reflections (maxdepth 1), shadows, directional lights, transformation (rotate by 90), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 36 seconds

Depth 10 Reflection

Features: triangles, spheres, Ambient, Diffuse, Specular with reflections (maxdepth 10), shadows, directional lights, transformation (rotate by 90), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 46 seconds

Trinormals and Ellipsoids

Features: trinormals, ellipsoids, Phong shading, reflections (maxdepth 10), shadows, directional and point lights, arbitrary transformations (rotations, scales, translations), writing out image to .png

Computation Time: 35 seconds

Planet Earth

Features: spheres, phong shading, shadows

Computation Time: 10 seconds
